03 — Project case studies

Selected work

01
✦ Product analytics · Fintech · SQL

KYC Funnel Analysis & Optimization

Analyzed user verification behavior across a 7-step KYC flow to identify critical drop-off points and improve completion rates. Built step-by-step conversion queries in SQL tracking signup → email → phone → ID → selfie → address → approved, segmented by country, device, and acquisition channel. Added time-to-verify percentile analysis (P50/P75/P90/P95) and rejection retry patterns to surface why users abandoned — not just where.

Key outcome
↗ Identified ID upload as the highest-abandonment step (76% drop-off). Rejection timing and device type were strongest predictors — surfaced actionable fixes for the product team.
SQL Funnel Analysis Cohort Analysis Segmentation

✦ Business intelligence · E-commerce · Power BI

E-commerce Product Performance Dashboard

Built an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing product metrics for an online store. Covered sales performance by category and SKU, customer segmentation and purchase patterns, a full conversion funnel (views → cart → purchase → returns), inventory turnover and stockout flags, geographic sales heatmaps, and time-series trends with forecasting. Designed for self-service use by non-technical stakeholders.

Key outcome
↗ Surfaced top 3 underperforming SKUs accounting for 22% of returns. Geographic heatmap revealed untapped regional demand for targeted campaign planning.

Product Pricing Optimization Model

Built a dynamic pricing model in Excel with full scenario analysis for a product line. Included price elasticity analysis with sensitivity tables, break-even analysis by product, competitive pricing comparison matrix, what-if scenarios using data tables and Goal Seek, profitability analysis with marginal cost calculations, and interactive dashboards with slicers and pivot tables.

Key outcome
↗ Model revealed a 12% price increase on two product lines was viable without demand loss — projected to add margin without volume sacrifice.
